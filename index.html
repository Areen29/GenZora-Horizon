Research Findings

Key themes identified through interviews, observations & qualitative analysis across 15 participants.

01

Frequent AI Usage

Students use AI chatbots daily for assignments, coding help, summarizing content, and exam preparation — seamlessly integrated into academic life.

Daily Interaction
02

Technical Limitations

Participants encountered inaccurate responses, subscription barriers, hallucinated answers, and context-window constraints that disrupted their workflow.

Pain Points
03

Human Emotional Support

Students strongly preferred human interaction for emotional concerns, mental health issues, and complex academic dilemmas requiring genuine empathy.

Human-Centric
04

AI as a Tool, Not a Replacement

All participants unanimously agreed: AI augments human capability but cannot replicate genuine empathy, lived experience, or nuanced human judgment.
